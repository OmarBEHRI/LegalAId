LegalAId: Your personal Moroccan legal guide powered by AI.
Easily access the intricacies of Moroccan law and legal procedures.

Purpose

Our mission at LegalAId is to democratize access to legal information in Morocco. We aim to break down barriers and provide comprehensive legal resources to all Moroccans, irrespective of language or educational background.

Vision

We envision a future where every Moroccan citizen, entrepreneur, and law student can confidently navigate the legal landscape. Through our AI-powered ChatBot and integrated platform, we aim to revolutionize legal information accessibility and foster a more informed society.

About LegalAId

LegalAId is a pioneering project initiated by a team of first-year IT students at ENSIAS, the National School of Computer Science and Systems Analysis. Our team comprises Omar BEHRI, specializing in Software Engineering, along with Yahya Moudrik and Mehdi LOUKILI, both focusing on Business Intelligence and Analytics.
